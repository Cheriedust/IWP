Notice

Dear Students,
As we gear up to welcome you to Freshers’25, we request your full cooperation in maintaining discipline and ensuring a safe and enjoyable event.
Kindly go through the following rules and regulations carefully:

1. Entry Timings: Entries will start from 6:00 PM sharp and will close by 9:00 PM.

2. Entry Gate: All colleges must enter from the main gate only.

3. Re-Entry: Re-entries will not be permitted under any circumstances.

4. ID Verification: Only valid physical University ID cards will be accepted for entry.

5. Intoxication: Any student found intoxicated or indulging in malpractice will be denied entry.

6. Prohibited Substances: Possession or consumption of intoxicating substances is strictly prohibited within the campus.

7. Security Protocols: Students must cooperate with frisking, bag scanning, and other security checks.

8. Liability: The University will not be held responsible for any injury or loss of personal belongings during the event.

9. Contrabands: Any person found with contraband or illegal substances will be debarred, and the matter will be handed over to the police.

10. Intoxicated Attendees: Students found in an intoxicated state will be denied entry, even with a valid ID card.


We request everyone to strictly adhere to these rules to make Freshers’25 a safe, memorable, and enjoyable celebration for all.

Looking forward to your cooperation.
